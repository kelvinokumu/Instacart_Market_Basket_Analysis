### Instacart Market Basket Analysis

#### Overview
The Instacart Market Basket Analysis project delves into customer shopping behavior using transactional data from the Instacart platform. This exploration offers insights to optimize operations and enhance customer experiences.

#### Data Analysis and Insights

1. **Market Basket Analysis**
   - *Analysis*: Uncover frequently co-occurring products to refine store layout and marketing strategies.
   - *Key Questions*:
     - Top 10 product pairs frequently purchased together.
     - Top 5 products commonly added to the cart first.
     - Typical count of unique products per order.

2. **Customer Segmentation**
   - *Analysis*: Group customers for targeted marketing based on purchasing behavior.
   - *Key Questions*:
     - Categorization of customers by total spending.
     - Different customer segments by purchase frequency.
     - Orders placed per customer.

3. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Prediction**
   - *Analysis*: Estimate potential customer value over their entire relationship.
   - *Key Questions*:
     - Average CLV across different customer segments.
     - CLV fluctuations over the past year.

4. **Seasonal Trends Analysis**
   - *Analysis*: Identify seasonal patterns in customer behavior and sales.
   - *Key Questions*:
     - Distribution of orders across different days of the week.
     - Months exhibiting higher order volumes.

5. **Customer Churn Prediction**
   - *Analysis*: Predict customers likely to stop using the service soon.
   - *Key Questions*:
     - Identification of customers inactive for 30 days.
     - Percentage of churned customers in the past quarter.

6. **Product Association Rules**
   - *Analysis*: Discover patterns in customer behavior regarding product associations.
   - *Key Questions*:
     - Top 5 product combinations frequently purchased together.
     - Products commonly bought together on weekends vs. weekdays.
